## The 8 Emotions Framework

Every high-performing post triggers one of these emotions (in order of frequency):

### OHHH (8 instances) - Most Common
**Trigger**: Bold claims, surprising shifts, unexpected data
**Examples**:
- "The Gen AI party is over"
- "The AI Talent Race Is On"
- "67% of organizations are creating new AI roles"

**When to Use**: Awareness content, trend shifts, industry changes

### WTF (4 instances)
**Trigger**: Contradictions, uncomfortable truths, unpopular opinions
**Examples**:
- "RPA hardly scaled"
- "Unpopular opinion: AI agents will replace most apps"
- "Everyone says X. They're wrong."

**When to Use**: Challenge status quo, controversial takes

### AWW (2 instances)
**Trigger**: Relatable personal anecdotes, vulnerability
**Examples**:
- "I lost 84% on UiPath"
- "I'm hearing stories about AI failures"
- "My journey wasn't easy"

**When to Use**: Personal stories, lessons learned

### YAY (2 instances)
**Trigger**: Helpful, actionable tips, simple frameworks
**Examples**:
- "Automation 101"
- "Here's your checklist"
- "5-step framework"

**When to Use**: Educational content, how-tos

### WOW (1 instance)
**Trigger**: Future dominance, bold predictions
**Examples**:
- "Careers dominating 2026"
- "The next wave of technology"

**When to Use**: Future-focused content, predictions

### LOL (1 instance)
**Trigger**: Humorous takes, satire
**Examples**:
- "Agentic AI circus"

**When to Use**: Light humor (use sparingly)

### FINALLY (Implied)
**Trigger**: Relief at solutions, breakthroughs
**When to Use**: Announcing solutions to known problems

### NSFW (Never Used)
**Trigger**: None
**When to Use**: Never

## Performance Scoring Model (50 Points Total)

### Component Weights
1. **Hook Type (30%)**: 0-10 points
2. **List/Listicle (25%)**: 0-10 points
3. **Questions/CTA (20%)**: 0-10 points
4. **Emoji Strategy (15%)**: 0-10 points
5. **Data/Stats (10%)**: 0-10 points

### Scoring Criteria

**Hook Type (0-10)**:
- Bold claim with urgency: 10 points
- Vulnerability/personal story: 7 points
- Contradiction: 7 points
- Specific results: 6 points
- Question opening: 5 points
- Generic opening: 3 points

**List/Listicle (0-10)**:
- Numbered list with 5+ items: 10 points
- Numbered list with 3-4 items: 8 points
- Bullet list with emojis: 7 points
- Simple bullet list: 5 points
- No structure: 3 points

**Questions/CTA (0-10)**:
- Specific question (role/skill/tool): 10 points
- Double question: 10 points
- Generic question: 6 points
- Question present but not at end: 4 points
- No question: 0 points

**Emoji Strategy (0-10)**:
- 2-4 semantic emojis: 10 points
- 5-6 emojis or 1 only: 6 points
- 7+ emojis or heavy decorative: 3 points
- No emojis: 5 points (neutral)

**Data/Stats (0-10)**:
- Percentage + cited source: 10 points
- Percentage only: 7 points
- Source only or neutral: 5 points

### Performance Prediction
- **Score 35-50**: High performance (>3% engagement, top 10%)
- **Score 25-35**: Moderate performance (1.5-3% engagement)
- **Score 0-25**: Low performance (<1.5% engagement, needs revision)
